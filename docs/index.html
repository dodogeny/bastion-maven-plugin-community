Get Started in 5 Minutes

Three simple steps to secure your Maven project

1 Add the Plugin

<!-- Add to your pom.xml --> <plugin> <groupId>io.github.dodogeny</groupId> <artifactId>bastion-maven-community-plugin</artifactId> <version>1.2.8-rc7</version> </plugin>

2 Run Your Build

$ mvn clean verify

3 Review Your Report

Open target/bastion-reports/bastion-report.html in your browser.

That's it! You're now scanning for vulnerabilities.
